Interpreting

Interpreting is important to the Deaf community as well. Interpreters relay information in ASL to Deaf individuals. They are there to listen to the hearing person and relay the message in ASL. It is important that interpreters are qualified and have a knowledge of the Deaf community. It is also important that the interpreter recognize that they are there for the Deaf person to provide a service, the are not better than that person, but they are there to serve them.
